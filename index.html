Recruiting Process

How player information gets updated

01

Athlete intake

Athletes complete one profile form with film, measurables, academics, awards, and approved public contact options like an X handle.

02

Staff review queue

Coaches review incoming records in the linked sheet, add staff notes, and set `publish` to `yes` only when a profile is ready for the public board.

03

Recruiter access

Recruiters browse the live board, open athlete profiles, watch Hudl, and use the approved contact path without needing separate spreadsheets or email chains.
